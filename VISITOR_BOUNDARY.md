# Φ-Cosmography · Visitor Boundary

This document defines what exists
before and after Cosmography.

## Before Cosmography

- Expectations
- Questions
- Goals
- Interpretations
- Desire for results

These do not belong to Cosmography.

## Inside Cosmography

- Structure
- Relations
- Invariants
- Boundaries

Nothing reacts.
Nothing responds.

## After Cosmography

- Interpretation (external)
- Meaning (external)
- Decisions (external)
- Action (external)

Cosmography does not continue beyond this boundary.

Rule:
Cosmography is a crossing, not a destination.
