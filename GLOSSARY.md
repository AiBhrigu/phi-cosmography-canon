# Φ-Cosmography · Glossary

This glossary defines terms used in Cosmography.
Definitions are structural, not interpretative.

## Canon
Causal foundation.
Defines invariants and structure.
Read-only.

## Light
Manifestation layer.
Does not affect Canon.

## Field
Contextual medium.
Enables coherent reading.
Does not act.

## Atlas
Registry layer.
Enumerates what exists.
No execution.

## Observer
Read-only reader.
No agency.
No memory.

## X-Domain
Boundary of unknown or external domains.
No content.
No influence.

## Reading Mode
State of interaction.
No response.
No mutation.

## Implicit Axis
Causal reference.
Not rendered.
Not a center.

Rule:
Terms describe position, not meaning.
